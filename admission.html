Admission and Enrollment

Basic Admission Requirements

Incoming Freshmen
o Form 138 (High School Card)
o Birth Certificate
o Good Moral Character Certificate
o View Procedure for Admission Application

Returning Students
o Evaluation form from the Registrar
o Account Slip

Foreign Students
Prior submission of the following document before enrollment
o Authenticated Transcript of Records
o National Intelligence Coordinating Agency (NICA) Clearance
o Quarantine Certificate
o Notice of Acceptance (NOA)
o Notarized Bureau of Immigration Form
o Notarized Authorization for processing of Student Visa by the School

Transferees
o Certificate of Honorable Dismissal or Transfer Credentials
o True Copy of Scholastic Records
o Good Moral Character Certificate

College of Law
o Official Transcript of Records with Special Order
o Entrance Examination Results

Graduate School
o Official Transcript of Records with Special Order
o True Copy of Grades
o PRC License for Board Exams
o Entrance Examination Results (for MSCJE)
